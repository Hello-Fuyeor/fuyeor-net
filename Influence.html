What is the "Influence for Search Engine"?

The purpose of this project is https://www.fuyeor.net/Influence.html The method of placing links on the page promotes the search engine to collect our latest social media pages, news information and other content as soon as possible, so that visitors can search our social media accounts through the search engine.

Because it is currently in the stage of information explosion and growth, the speed of these pages is obviously not satisfactory only depending on the search engine itself to access and crawl through the link relationship layer by layer. Therefore, we need to reduce the level of link relationships, namely - through fuyeor.net - direct discovery.
